Questions

Answers before you hop on the waitlist.

Which marketplaces does Encore monitor?

Today we support Depop, Vinted, Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, and select boutique forums. More regions unlock monthly.

Do I need to share my login credentials?

No. We only use publicly available listing data and never request personal passwords or session tokens.

How do notifications work?

Choose SMS, email, or Slack and set quiet hours. Alerts are rate limited so you only see the deals that matter.

What does pricing look like?

Plans start with a free scouting tier and scale based on the number of sourcing lanes and team seats you activate.
